Creating a person

How does it work?

The

AI

is tasked with creating an image of a person, and it may be provided with some

specifications

.

These may be as simple as

"A firefighter"

or a list of specifications

"Blonde, Woman, Black eyglasses, sad..."

.

Why do this?

To train a

facial recognition software

such as the one that most phones use, without the need for a lot of real people.

To remain anonymous by creating

"an avatar"

instead of providing a real photo of yourself.

What to look out for?

Do not assume

that everyone you communicate with online is a real person just because they have a picture on social media account.

Do not trust

everyone just because you assume you know what they look like.

How does it work?

Face swapping might spoof some

How can it work in practice?

Scenario 1:

  1. The attacker finds the victim's social media account, which gives them access to the victim's photos, additional accounts, personal information, etc.

  2. The attacker searches for pornographic images of someone whose body resembles that of the victim, or synthesizes new ones.

  3. The attacker creates deepfakes of the victim and then proceeds to blackmail them with these manipulated images, threatening to leak the content unless certain demands are met.

Scenario 2:

  1. One classmate is bullying another, and the victim decides to retaliate by taking steps to get the bully kicked out of school.

  2. As classmates, obtaining a photo of the aggressor is easy for the target of bullying.

  3. Subsequently, they create a face-swapped deepfake using a picture of the bully and an image of a person engaged in alcohol and smoking activities.

  4. The victim then creates a new email to anonymously send the deepfake to their school, resulting in disciplinary measures for the tormentor.

Scenario 3:

  1. An entitled parent is dissatisfied with the teacher's treatment of their child.

  2. The parent downloads images of the teacher from the school's website and nude images of a person resembling them.

  3. They use this material to create a deepfake, superimposing the teacher's face onto the explicit images.

  4. Afterward, the parent shares the manipulated content to accuse the teacher of inappropriate behavior, either with other parents or via a complaint with the school, demanding the teacher's dismissal.

FACE MANIPULATED

How does it work?

A source image and list of manipulated features is provided to the AI.

The features may be added, changed or removed.

Things like hair colour or expression can be changed and eyeglasses or tattoos can be added.

Why do this?

To make better or more funny photos without the need for photoshop.

Save money on a make-up artist.

What to look out for?

Strangers can manipulate your photos and for example make you look drunk or add an unwanted feature such as a tattoo.

It is easy to

spread disinformation

online with manipulated images of people.

How can it work in practice?

Scenario 1:

  1. An internationally wanted criminal needs to enter a country via plane, without being detected by the authorities.

  2. They ask a pal to lend them their ID and a photo, in order to make a new passport.

  3. The criminal takes photo of themselves and of their pal and morphs these photos together to create a deepfake photo, which will be put onto the new passport.

  4. Armed with photo and legitimate ID of a non-wanted person, the criminal can legitimately make a new passport.

  5. Once the passport arrives, the criminal can board any plane and use the automated border control system to enter a country inconspicuously.

Scenario 2:

  1. A corporate spy aims to infiltrate a rival corporation to gather sensitive information.

  2. The spy searches the company's website for employee photos and selects a target whose role aligns with their objective.

  3. Using advanced face-morphing technology, the spy creates a convincing deepfake photo by blending their own facial features with the chosen employee's.

  4. Armed with the face-morphed image, the spy leverages social networks to gather additional information about the target's role, habits, and interactions within the company.

  5. Infiltrating the corporation, the spy navigates undetected, exploiting the face-morphed photo for security clearance and accessing confidential information.
